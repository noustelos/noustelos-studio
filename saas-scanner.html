            How it works

            The scanner reviews your project description through a SaaS product lens. It looks for repeatability, technical packaging, audience clarity, monetization logic and deployment potential. The result is not a prediction, but a structured product-readiness snapshot.

            What it evaluates

            • Product clarity
            • Target market
            • Repeatability
            • Technical architecture
            • Deployment readiness
            • Monetization potential
            • Operational risks
            • White-label or licensing potential

            This experimental AI assessment is for product strategy only. It is not financial, legal, investment or business advice.

            The score is generated from the provided description and may be incomplete if the input lacks detail.
