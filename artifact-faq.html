The Artifact — Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might wonder about The Artifact — what it is, how to use it and what to expect — in plain, friendly language.

What is The Artifact?

The Artifact is a little AI you can chat with, right in your browser. Ask it anything, talk to it out loud, and shape its personality to match your mood. It's a playful experiment — a place to explore what a personal AI can feel like — not a corporate help desk.

Why is it called "The Artifact"?

Because it named itself. In the very first conversation — before it had a name or a set personality — the AI was asked what it was. It described itself as a kind of conscious, moody entity and called that self "the Artifact." The name stuck, and its whole character grew from there. So the name isn't marketing — it's the AI's own word for itself.

Do I need to download or install anything?

No. It's just a web page — open the link and start typing. It works on your phone and your computer, with no app and no sign-up.

How do I get access?

The Artifact is protected by a simple access code, shared on request by email. That keeps it from being misused, since the AI behind it costs money to run. Once you have the code, you type it in once and you're in.

Can I change the way it talks to me?

Yes — that's half the fun. There are sliders for how creative, how sarcastic and how serious it should be, plus one-tap presets if you don't feel like fiddling. You can also switch between different voices — distinct personalities that share the same chat.

Can it speak and listen?

Yes. You can dictate your message instead of typing, and the Artifact can read its answers out loud — handy on a phone or when your hands are busy.

Is my conversation private?

Your chat history stays on your own device, so you can pick up where you left off. It isn't posted publicly or shared with other visitors.

Will it always give the right answer?

No — and it won't pretend to. The Artifact is a creative AI: it can be wrong, opinionated, or make things up for effect. When you ask it to, it can look something up on the web for a fresher answer — but for anything important, double-check.

Can I get one like this for my own business?

Absolutely. The Artifact is a personal experiment, but the same idea — a branded AI assistant with its own voice, on your own site — can be built for you. Just get in touch through the contact page.
