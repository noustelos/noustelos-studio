About

I'm Nick Karadimas, a creative web designer and developer based in Santorini.

I build custom websites and landing pages for tourism, hospitality, local businesses and creative projects. My work combines clear structure, visual storytelling, responsive design and practical functionality.

I use AI-assisted workflows to move faster, explore stronger directions and refine details, but every project is shaped through human judgment, taste and final quality control. The goal is not to produce another template. The goal is to create a site that feels specific, useful and well-directed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Noustelos Studio?

Noustelos Studio is a web design and development practice based in Santorini, Greece, led by Nick Karadimas. It builds custom, AI-assisted websites and landing pages for tourism, hospitality, local businesses and creative projects.

What services does Noustelos Studio offer?

Custom website design, focused landing pages, creative web projects, and agentic AI platforms delivered as SaaS — with a focus on tourism and hospitality.

What is AskSantorini.ai?

AskSantorini.ai is the studio's flagship live product: a free, AI-powered guide for Santorini visitors that answers practical questions about beaches, sunsets, tours, food and transport.
