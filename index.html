About

I'm Nick Karadimas, a creative web designer and developer based in Santorini.

I build custom websites and landing pages for tourism, hospitality, local businesses and creative projects. My work combines clear structure, visual storytelling, responsive design and practical functionality.

I use AI-assisted workflows to move faster, explore stronger directions and refine details, but every project is shaped through human judgment, taste and final quality control. The goal is not to produce another template. The goal is to create a site that feels specific, useful and well-directed.
