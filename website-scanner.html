            How it works

            The scanner fetches your URL and extracts real public page signals — title, meta tags, headings, links, images, content and structured data — then reviews them through a practical launch and conversion lens: clarity, trust, SEO basics, content completeness and the missing material that may prevent a good site from becoming a stronger business asset. It reports only on what it can actually see in the page; it does not guess. If the page can't be fetched, it tells you instead of inventing a score.

            What it evaluates

            • Visual trust
            • Message clarity
            • SEO readiness
            • Conversion flow
            • Trust signals
            • Content completeness
            • Technical basics
            • Client-dependent improvements

            This experimental AI assessment is for website strategy only. It is not a full technical audit, SEO guarantee, legal advice, financial advice or ranking prediction.

            The report is based on public page signals fetched from the URL you provide (server-rendered HTML only) plus the information you add. It is not a full crawl, a Lighthouse / Core Web Vitals measurement or a ranking prediction. If the page cannot be fetched, no scored report is produced.
