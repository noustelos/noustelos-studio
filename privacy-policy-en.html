Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03/04/2026

Noustelos Studio respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is processed when you use noustelos.gr.

1. Data controller

The data controller is Noustelos Studio. For any data protection request, contact info@noustelos.gr.

2. What data we collect

When browsing noustelos.gr, the following data may be collected:

  • Information you voluntarily send by email (name, email, request details).
  • Basic technical connection data (such as IP address, user-agent, timestamp) through hosting provider logs for security and site reliability.
  • User preferences stored locally in your browser (such as content language and cookie banner choice).

3. Purpose and legal basis of processing

Data processing is carried out for:

  • Responding to contact requests and delivering services (GDPR Article 6(1)(b)).
  • Security, technical stability, and abuse prevention (GDPR Article 6(1)(f) - legitimate interest).
  • Compliance with legal obligations where required (GDPR Article 6(1)(c)).

4. Cookies and local storage

This site uses only essential cookies or equivalent local storage for preference functions (for example, language selection and cookie banner preference). No advertising or profiling cookies are used.

5. Data retention

Communication data is retained only for as long as needed to serve your request and comply with any legal obligations. Technical logs are retained for a limited period according to the hosting provider's policy.

6. Recipients and transfers

Access to data is limited to authorized persons and technical providers (for example, hosting) only when needed for site operation. Personal data is not sold.

7. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and data portability where applicable. You can exercise your rights via email at info@noustelos.gr.

8. Supervisory authority

If you believe that processing of your personal data violates applicable law, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (www.dpa.gr).

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The latest version is always available at https://noustelos.gr/privacy-policy-en.html.
